Who We Are

A Small Business That Truly Cares

Purposefully Made KC was born from a simple belief: custom apparel should feel as good as it looks, and the process of ordering it should be just as enjoyable. We are a Kansas City-based small business that puts real care into every single order.

We use only premium Bella+Canvas garments paired with state-of-the-art DTF (Direct-to-Film) printing to give you colors that are vibrant, prints that last, and shirts that actually feel amazing on. Whether you are ordering one shirt or one hundred, you will feel the difference.

This is not just a transaction for us. When you order with Purposefully Made KC, you are trusting us with something that matters to you, and we take that seriously every time.
